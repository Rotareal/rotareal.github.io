Rotareal Compression Tester RLCT-01

The RLCT-01 digital rotary compression tester by Rotareal is the simple, reliable, and low power rotary compression tester. Powered by the ATMEL ATMEGA328P microcontroller the open source RLCT-01 provides a simple computer aided compression testing experience. Simply run the RLCT Monitor tool on your laptop, connect via USB to the RLCT-01 and connect the included pressure sensor to the trailing spark plug hole and test away!

  • RLCT-01-C Controller - The brains behind the compression tester processing data produced by the pressure sensor to give you cranking speed and compression readings for all three rotor faces.
  • RLCT-SNS01 Sensor - This high pressure rated pressure transducer allows us to read compression data without the need for a manual gauge.

Rotareal Smart Compression Tester RLCT-S

EFor those of us serious concerned with the health of our rotary engines we are developing the Rotareal Smart Compression Tester. This tester is an all-in-one unit that reliably tests your engine's compression and store's the logged data on the built in storage. Connect the tester to WiFi to upload compression data to Rotareal's RotaryDB for a bigger picture of your engine's health. Firmware upgrades will also be offered as they come to provide the most accurate, most secure compression testing experience.

Compression Cores

  • RLCT-S-MTU - The RLCT-S Master Testing Unit processes, saves, and analyzes compression data from the pressure interface unit. This core unit has embedded WiFi connectivity that can be easily updated for more reliable testing data.
  • RLCT-S-PIU - The RLCT-S Pressure Interface Unit works with a variety of RLCT sensors to read compression data to be sent back to the MTU. While this unit has very minimal complexity there are times when precision can be increased and test time can be minimized with altered firmware. Or, whenever switching out pressure sensors you can keep the same PIU. Firmware is updated live over wire through the MTU. Firmware files for this unit are not  directly available but are included in updates to the MTU.

Compression Sensor Options

  • RLCT-SNS01 (Option 1) - This high pressure rated pressure transducer allows us to read compression data without the need for a manual gauge.
  • RLCT-SNS04 (Option 2) - An upgraded pressure transducer powered by Honeywell allows for even more precision in compression testing while showing proven reliability advantages over SNS01.
  • Alternate Sensors (Option 3) - Rotareal also offers several other sensors with specific advantages that are compatible with the RLCT-S-PIU available soon.
